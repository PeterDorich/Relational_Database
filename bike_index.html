Welcome to The Bike Shop Main Menu

This site allows you to reserve a bike rental for today!
If you haven't already, go ahead and click the "Sign-up" tab, where you'll create your account.

Then, go to the "Log-In" page to sign in.

After you sign in, you can visit the "Rent" tab to select a bike from our list!

Once you've made a rental(s), go to your "Account Settings" page to verify that everything is correct.

If you made a mistake on your rental, you can edit your rental and choose another bike!

If you would like to cancel your order, you can do that from the "account settings" page as well.

After that, we'll see you at the shop!
